Austin's Latte Recipe

How to make the best home brewed latte ever:

This receipe may take some time to learn and acquire the equipment for, but you will be brewing the best coffee just like your favorite barista in no time.

Ingredients:

  • 15 grams of fresh coffee beans.
  • 250 ml of appropriate espresso grade water.

Preparation:

  1. Acquire expensive espresso machine.
  2. Buy very nice beans and weigh out 15 grams.
  3. Grind said beans using the world's fanciest grinder.
  4. Tamp grounds in portafilter using WDT tool and tamper.
  5. Attach portafilter to espresso machine and turn on.
  6. Brew for ~30 seconds or when weight of coffee is 30 grams.
  7. Steam milk until desired consistency met.
  8. Make latte art using steamed milk and foam.
  9. enjoy!
