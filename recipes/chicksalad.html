Chicken Salad Chick Recipe (Original Recipe)

Chicken Salad Chick's Classic Carol:

Ranch dressing seasoning is the secret ingredient in this easy to prepare chicken salad that goes well over a kale salad for a simple and healthy lunch

Ingredients:

  • 1lb chicken breast or thighs.
  • 1 packet Hidden Valley ranch dressing seasoning.
  • 1 cup mayonaise.
  • 2/3 cup sour cream or greek yogurt.
  • 1/4 cup olive oil.
  • Foil.
  • Salt and pepper to taste.

Preparation:

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper and olive oil and wrap in foil.
  2. Cook chicken in foil packets in over at 350 degrees.
  3. While chicken is cooking, combine ranch seasoning, mayo, greek yogurt, and remaining olive oil.
  4. Once chicken is out of oven and cooled slightly, cut up into large cubes and add to bowl of stand mixer.
  5. Add mayo mix to chicken in stand mixer and slowly increase speed until well shredded and dressing is incorporated.
  6. enjoy!
