Death and Co Negroni Recipe (Link)

Death and co's World Famous Negroni:

For years Death and Co has been a world renowned bar. This recipe brings their negroni cocktail to your home bar with minimal effort!

Ingredients:

  • 2.25 ounces London Beefeater Gin.
  • 2.25 ounces Dolin Rouge Vermouth.
  • 2.25 ounces Campari bitters.
  • Orange Angustura bitters.
  • Lemon.
  • Ice.

Preparation:

  1. Measure one part each of gin, vermouth, and Campari bitters and pour into mixer.
  2. Add a dash of Angustura bitters and ice.
  3. Use long handle stirrer and stir ingredients until well diluted.
  4. Strain into a high-ball glass with one or two large block ice pieces.
  5. Express lemon over drink and add as garnish.
  6. enjoy!
